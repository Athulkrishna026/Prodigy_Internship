MACHINES

MARBLE CUTTER

A marble cutter is a specialized power tool, typically a diamond-blade saw or grinder, used for cutting, shaping, and polishing marble, granite, and other stone materials with precision and control.

JIG SAW

A jigsaw is a versatile power tool used for curved or irregular cuts in various materials, such as wood, metal, or plastic, by moving a reciprocating blade up and down.

JACK HAMMER

A jackhammer is a heavy-duty pneumatic or electric tool used for breaking up, demolishing, or drilling through hard materials like concrete, asphalt, or rock.

CAR WASH

A power washer is a high-pressure cleaning tool that uses a powerful stream of water to remove dirt, grime, and stains from surfaces such as driveways, sidewalks, buildings, and equipment.
